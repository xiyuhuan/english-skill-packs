### 活动2：情境侦探（5题）

**规则：** 读情境描述，猜应填入哪个核心词汇

**题目示例：**
1. "Every autumn, monarch butterflies ________ thousands of miles south."
   → migrate / travel long distances
2. "Scientists discovered that monarchs use the sun for ________."
   → navigation
3. "The use of chemicals can ________ a decline in butterfly numbers."
   → lead to
4. "Monarchs ________ because their habitats are being destroyed."
   → are in danger
5. "Farmers ________ milkweed plants..."
   → cut down

## Step 2：阅读理解（Three-Read Strategy）

### 一读：Skim — 主题打卡

**判断题：**
1. The passage is mainly about how beautiful monarch butterflies are.（X）
2. The passage discusses both the migration of monarchs and the threats they face.（✓）
3. The passage mentions that humans are helping to protect monarchs.（✓）
4. The passage only talks about butterflies, not other animals.（X）

**主题判断：**
| 主题 | ✓ 或 X |
|------|--------|
| migration 迁徙 | ✓ |
| trouble 困境 | ✓ |
| human activity 人类活动 | ✓ |
| protection 保护行动 | ✓ |

---

### 二读：Scan — 段落拼图

**打乱顺序的段落主旨：**
- A. 科学家破解了帝王蝶的导航奥秘
- B. 人类的保护行动正在守护帝王蝶的未来
- C. 帝王蝶遭遇严重生存危机，数量锐减
- D. 引出动物迁徙，帝王蝶是自然界最奇妙的迁徙者之一

**正确答案：** D → A → C → B（即 Para.1 → Para.2-3 → Para.4 → Para.5）

---

### 三读：Read for Details — 地图闯关

#### 站点 1：加拿大·出发地 → Para.1

**Q1:** What is migration according to the passage?

**提示：**
- 注意第一段中出现了 "regular movement"
- 想想动物为什么要"搬家"

**答案：** The regular movement of animals from one place to another.

---

#### 站点 2：美国中部·4000公里的旅途中 → Para.2-3

**Q2a:** How do monarchs find their way without a map?

**提示：**
- 内置时钟 + 太阳指南针
- time-compensated sun compass

**答案：** They use the position of the sun as a compass and an internal biological clock.

**Q2b（高阶）：** Compare monarchs' navigation with another animal.
> Use: "Like monarchs, [animal] also navigates by..."

---

#### 站点 3：墨西哥·越冬栖息地 → Para.4

**Q3:** Why are monarchs in trouble now?

**答案：** habitats destroyed, milkweed disappearing, climate change

**数据卡片：**
| 数据 | 含义 |
|------|------|
| 📉 20年间下降约80% | 种群数量锐减 |
| 🪓 越冬森林缩小75% | 栖息地被破坏 |
| 🧪 杀虫剂 | 导致乳草大量消失 |

**追问：** 这些数字给你什么感受？用一句话形容。

---

#### 站点 4：保护行动·终点也是新起点 → Para.5

**Q4:** What can we do to help monarchs?

**参考行动：**
| 保护措施 | 具体行动 |
|----------|----------|
| 🌱 | 在迁徙路线上种植乳草 |
| 🚫 | 减少杀虫剂使用 |
| 🌲 | 保护墨西哥越冬森林 |
| 📢 | 教育更多人了解帝王蝶 |

---

#### 闯关总结

**问题：** Why do scientists find the monarch's navigation ability amazing?

**答案：** Because a single butterfly weighs less than 1 gram, yet it can fly 4,000 km to exactly the same trees its great-grandparents visited, using only the sun and its internal clock — no GPS, no map.

## Step 4：讨论升华

**话题：** "The more we know about this lovely creature, the greater the chance it will survive."

**三个Level：**
- 🟢 Level 1（理解层）："了解"为什么能带来"保护"？
- 🟡 Level 2（迁移层）：这个道理还能套用在哪些濒危生物上？
- 🔴 Level 3（行动层）：作为高中生，你能做的一件保护动物的小事是什么？

**金句：**
1. "We don't inherit the earth from our ancestors; we borrow it from our children."
2. "In the end, we will conserve only what we love, we will love only what we understand." — Baba Dioum
3. "Every species matters. The loss of one is the loss of a piece of ourselves."
