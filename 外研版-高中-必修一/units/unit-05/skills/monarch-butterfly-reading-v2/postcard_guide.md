# 明信片创作指南 (Postcard Guide)

## 概述

本文档为《The Monarch's Journey》AI课堂教学中的明信片创作环节提供语言支架和词汇支持。

---

## 明信片模板

```
┌────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  📮 POSTCARD                           │
│                                        │
│  From: [你的名字/帝王蝶的名字]          │
│  Location: [你现在的位置]              │
│  Date: [今天的日期]                    │
│                                        │
│  Dear Friend,                          │
│                                        │
│  [正文内容：描述你的迁徙经历，          │
│   你看到的风景，你遇到的挑战，          │
│   你想对人类说的话...]                  │
│                                        │
│  [至少40词，表达真情实感]               │
│                                        │
│  Best wishes,                           │
│  [署名]                                │
│  🦋                                     │
└────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## 段落模板

### 模板1：叙述型

```
Dear Friend,

I am a monarch butterfly. I [从哪里来/是谁]. This year, I [做了什么/经历了什么]. 
The journey was [形容旅程]. I saw [看到什么]. It made me feel [感受].

Now I am in [现在在哪里]. The forest is [形容环境]. I am [现在在做什么/感受]. 
Thank you [感谢原因]. 

Please [呼吁/请求].

Best wishes,
🦋 [署名]
```

### 模板2：感谢型

```
Dear Friend,

I want to say thank you. Thank you for [感谢的事情]. Because of you, [带来的改变].
I come from [来自哪里]. Every year, I make the long journey to [去哪里].

Please remember: [想说的话]. Together, we can make the world better for [生物/地球].

I hope [愿望].

With love,
🦋 [署名]
```

### 模板3：呼吁型

```
Dear Friend,

I am writing this postcard from [位置]. The view here is [形容景色].
But I am worried. [担心的原因].

We need your help. Please [请求].
Together, we can [可以做到的事情].

I believe that [信念/希望].

Take care of our beautiful world,
🦋 [署名]
```

## 优秀范文

### 范文1：叙述型（60词）

```
Dear Friend,

I am a monarch butterfly. Every autumn, I travel from Canada to Mexico. 
This year, I flew 4,000 kilometers under the blue sky. It was hard, but the wind 
helped me. Now I am in the beautiful forests of Mexico. Millions of my family 
members are here with me. 

Thank you for protecting our home!

Best wishes,
🦋 Orange Wing
```

### 范文2：感谢型（75词）

```
Dear Friend,

I want to say thank you. Thank you for planting more milkweed in your gardens. 
Thank you for creating sanctuaries for us. Because of your help, more monarch 
butterflies are surviving today.

I come from Canada. Every year, I make the long journey south. When I arrive 
in Mexico, I feel safe and happy. 

Please keep protecting nature. Together, we can make a difference!

With love,
🦋 Sunny
```

### 范文3：呼吁型（90词）

```
Dear Friend,

I am writing this postcard from the mountains of Mexico. The trees here are 
covered with orange butterflies like me. It is a beautiful sight. But I am 
worried. Climate change, pesticides, and loss of habitat are making our journey 
harder every year.

We need your help. Please plant milkweed in your gardens. Please reduce the 
use of harmful chemicals. Please tell others about our story.

The more we know about this lovely creature, the greater the chance it will 
survive.

I hope to see you someday!

Best wishes,
🦋 Esperanza (which means "hope" in Spanish)
```

## 写作技巧

### 技巧1：使用感官词
- **视觉：** see, watch, look, colorful, bright, beautiful
- **听觉：** hear, listen, sound
- **触觉：** feel, soft, warm, cool, gentle
- **嗅觉：** smell, fresh, sweet

### 技巧2：使用形容词
- **描述风景：** beautiful, amazing, colorful, peaceful, magical, wonderful
- **描述感受：** happy, tired, excited, grateful, worried, hopeful

### 技巧3：使用连接词
- **顺序：** first, then, next, finally
- **原因：** because, because of, so, that's why
- **对比：** but, however, although, even though
- **补充：** also, too, and, as well

### 技巧4：使用比喻
- Examples:
  - "The butterflies cover the trees like an orange blanket."
  - "The wind is like a gentle hand carrying us."
  - "The forest is our winter palace."

## 创作小贴士

1. **选择你的视角：** 你可以是任何一只帝王蝶，给自己起一个名字！
2. **想象你的旅程：** 想想你看到了什么，感受到了什么
3. **表达你的心声：** 你想对人类说什么？想感谢什么？想呼吁什么？
4. **使用学到的词汇：** 尽量在明信片中用到今天学的新单词
5. **写出真情实感：** 最好的作品来自内心！
